## Jan 14 (Tue) — Arrival

- **Evening:** Check-in, short walk Freeman Alley / Rivington St
- **Dinner:** Cafe Mogador (primary) | Russ & Daughters Cafe (fallback)
- **Optional:** McSorley's (one drink) — Sleep ~21:30

---

## Jan 15 (Wed) — Katz's + Brooklyn

- **Breakfast:** Coffee + pastry (hotel) | Tompkins Square Bagels (fallback)
- **11:00am:** Katz's Delicatessen — share 1 pastrami + 1 side
  - *TIP: Get ticket at door, DON'T LOSE IT ($50 fee). Tip cutter $2-5 cash. Ask for "fatty" pastrami.*
- **1:00pm:** Subway to Brooklyn Bridge (High St station)
- **1:30-4:00pm:** Walk Brooklyn Bridge → DUMBO → Brooklyn Heights Promenade
- **~4:30pm:** Walk back across bridge or subway
- **Dinner:** Joe's Pizza — **Fulton St location** (124 Fulton St) — 5 min from Brooklyn Bridge

---

## Jan 16 (Thu) — SoHo & West Village + Hamilton

- **Morning:** Sleep in, coffee + pastry near hotel
- **11am-3pm:** SoHo → West Village wandering (no agenda)
- **Optional 2pm:** Tenement Museum (one guided tour, book ahead)
- **5:00pm:** Arrive Spicy Village (Chinatown) for early dinner | Taiwan Pork Chop House (fallback)
- **6:15pm:** Subway to Times Square (42nd St)
- **7:20pm:** Arrive Richard Rodgers Theatre
- **8:00pm:** Hamilton
- **~10:45pm:** Post-show drinks — Ardesia Wine Bar (primary) | Times Square area if tired

---

## Jan 17 (Fri) — WTC + Met + Central Park + Jazz

- **9:00am:** Coffee near hotel or grab near WTC
- **10:00am:** 9/11 Memorial + Museum (~2 hrs) — less crowded early
- **12:30pm:** Subway uptown to Upper East Side
- **1:00pm:** Light lunch near Met
- **1:30-4:00pm:** The Metropolitan Museum of Art (max 2-3 sections)
  - *$30 or pay-what-you-wish for NY/NJ/CT residents*
- **4:00pm:** Central Park walk (The Mall → Bethesda Terrace → Bow Bridge)
- **5:30pm:** Head to Greenwich Village
- **6:30pm:** Dinner at Minetta Tavern | Balthazar (fallback) — **RESERVATION REQUIRED**
- **9:00pm:** Jazz — Village Vanguard | Smalls Jazz Club

---

## Jan 18 (Sat) — Departure

- **Morning:** Coffee / light bite — Abraco | Culture Espresso
